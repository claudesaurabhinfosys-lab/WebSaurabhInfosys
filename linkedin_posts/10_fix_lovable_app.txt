Your Lovable app is broken. Here is what to check first.

80% of the time it is one of these five things:

1. Blank white screen after deploy
The app works in Lovable preview but not on your domain. This is almost always a routing issue. Add a _redirects file to Netlify or configure your server to serve index.html for all paths.

2. Supabase 401 errors
Your VITE_SUPABASE_URL or VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY is wrong or missing. Check Lovable Project Settings and match them exactly to Supabase Project Settings.

3. Empty data (RLS is blocking you)
You can see the data in Supabase but your app returns nothing. Row Level Security is on by default. Add a SELECT policy to your table.

4. Auth redirect going to the wrong URL
Supabase only redirects to URLs on your allowed list. Add your production domain to Supabase Authentication URL Configuration.

5. AI going in circles
If you have tried the same fix 10 times and it keeps breaking, the problem is architectural. The AI cannot see the full picture. A developer can.
