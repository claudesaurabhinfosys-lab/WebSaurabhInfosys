RAG is the most underused AI technology in business right now. Here is what it is and why it matters.

RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation. In plain English: it lets an AI answer questions using your actual documents, not generic internet knowledge.

Without RAG: You ask a chatbot about your product. It makes up an answer based on training data. It is wrong 30% of the time and confident 100% of the time.

With RAG: You ask the same question. The system searches your actual product documentation, pricing sheet, and FAQ database. It gives a specific, accurate answer sourced from your own content.

Real business applications:

Customer support bot that knows your exact return policy, product specs, and service terms - not a generic version of them.

Internal knowledge base that lets staff query HR policies, technical documentation, and company procedures in plain English instead of searching through folders.

Sales assistant that can pull product comparisons, case studies, and pricing from your CRM and pitch documents on demand.

E-commerce search that understands "I need something for oily skin under $30" and retrieves matching products rather than keyword matches.
