Six signs your Lovable or Bolt app needs a real developer:

1. You have been on the same bug for more than 2 hours
If you have asked the AI to fix the same thing ten different ways and it is still broken, the problem is architectural. The AI cannot see the full picture. A developer can.

2. Fixing one thing breaks something else every time
Tight coupling. The AI makes local fixes without understanding the wider impact. Needs a proper refactor, not more patching.

3. You need real payment processing
Stripe webhooks, idempotency, duplicate order prevention, security practices. AI tools get this wrong regularly. Payment bugs cost real money.

4. You are building something regulated
Healthcare, finance, legal. AI is not trained to think about HIPAA, PDPA, or PCI-DSS.

5. You have real users and real data
A security vulnerability stops being theoretical the moment real data is in your database.

6. Performance is noticeably bad
Slow pages, queries timing out, crashes under load. Engineering problems that need profiling, not prompting.

The rule: once you have spent more time fighting the AI than a developer would need to fix it, you have crossed the line.

Most focused fixes take 2 to 6 hours. It is not as expensive as the days you are currently spending.
