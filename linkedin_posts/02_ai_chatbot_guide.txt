90% of AI chatbots built for businesses fail. Here is why.

They are trained on nothing relevant, hallucinate constantly, and frustrate customers more than they help them.

A good business AI chatbot does three things:

1. Knows your actual product, service, and pricing - not just generic information from the internet.
2. Knows when it cannot answer and hands off to a human without making the customer repeat themselves.
3. Integrates with your actual systems - your CRM, your booking calendar, your inventory - so it can take action, not just talk.

Building this right requires:
- Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) so the bot answers from your actual data
- Proper system prompt design so it stays on-brand and on-topic
- Integration with your existing tools via API
- Testing against real customer queries before go-live
