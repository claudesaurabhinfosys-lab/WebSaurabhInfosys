Clients ask us every week: should we build native iOS and Android apps or go cross-platform?

In 2025, for 90% of business applications, the answer is Flutter.

Here is the honest breakdown:

Native (Swift/Kotlin): Right if you need deep hardware integration, AR features, or are building something like a camera app or a game. Costs 2x and takes 2x longer because you build everything twice.

Flutter: One codebase, both platforms, same performance for most business apps. We have shipped Flutter apps with 30,000+ users across 20 countries. The users cannot tell it is not native.

What Flutter cannot do well: apps that need heavy platform-specific APIs or access to very new iOS/Android features the day they release.

What Flutter does better than native: faster development, consistent UI across platforms, single team maintains one codebase, cheaper to update.

For service businesses, booking platforms, marketplaces, fitness apps, logistics tools, and anything driven by data and UI - Flutter is the right choice.
